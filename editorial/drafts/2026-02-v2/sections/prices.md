## EU27 chemical prices decline 2.8% year-on-year in February 2026

EU27 chemical producer prices declined by 2.8% in February 2026 compared to February 2025, with the index reaching 117.2 (2021=100). In the first two months of 2026, chemical prices averaged 117.0, down 0.8% compared to the same period in 2025.

Chemical prices fell more sharply than total manufacturing, which edged up by 0.2% year-on-year in February. Pharmaceuticals rose by 1.9%, while basic metals surged by 8.8% and motor vehicles increased by 0.9%.

The Netherlands experienced the steepest price decline at 9.2%, followed by Belgium (5.5%) and France (3.0%). Germany reported a 1.7% decrease, Spain fell by 2.8%, and Poland declined by 1.9%. Italy showed the smallest decrease at 0.3%.
