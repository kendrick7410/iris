## EU27 chemical sales decline by 5.7% in 2025

EU27 chemical sales decreased by 5.7% in 2025 compared to 2024, with the index reaching 91.9 in December 2025. The sector underperformed total manufacturing, which grew by 2.1% over the same period.

Belgium experienced the steepest sales decline at 10.8%, followed by the Netherlands (6.9%) and Germany (5.6%). Spain reported the smallest decline at 2.4%, while Poland was the only country to post growth at 0.8%. France declined by 3.4% and Italy by 4.4%.

Chemical sales fell more sharply than other industrial sectors. While pharmaceuticals surged by 9.1% and basic metals climbed by 4.6%, chemicals declined by 5.7% compared to a marginal 1.1% decrease in motor vehicles. These chemical sales trends by country show a fragmented Europe.
