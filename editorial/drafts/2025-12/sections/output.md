## EU27 chemical output edges up 0.9% in 2025, remaining 16.8% below pre-crisis levels

EU27 chemical production increased by 0.9% in 2025 compared to 2024, reaching an index level of 80.5. The sector remains 16.8% below pre-crisis levels (2014–2019 average), underperforming total manufacturing which grew by 2.4% in 2025.

Poland reported the strongest growth at 3.1%, followed by Spain which remained stable at 0.0%. Germany experienced the steepest decline at 4.2%, followed by Belgium (4.5%) and Italy (3.6%). France and the Netherlands posted modest decreases of 0.4% and 1.9% respectively. These chemical production trends by country show a fragmented Europe.
