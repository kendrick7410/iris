## EU27 chemical output remains 18.0% below pre-crisis levels in February 2026

EU27 chemical production stood at 79.3 in February 2026, down by 3.9% compared to February 2025. In the first two months of 2026, chemical output averaged 79.1, declining by 1.7% compared to the same period in 2025.

The sector remains 18.0% below pre-crisis levels (2014–2019 average). Chemical production underperformed total manufacturing, which declined by 0.4% year-on-year in February 2026, while pharmaceuticals fell more, at 8.6%.
