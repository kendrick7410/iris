## EU27 chemical output down 3.9% in February 2026

EU27 chemical production declined by 3.9% in February 2026 compared to February 2025, with the index reaching 79.3. The sector remains 18.0% below pre-crisis levels (2014–2019 average). In the first two months of 2026, chemical output averaged 79.1, down 1.5% compared to the same period in 2025.

Chemical production underperformed total manufacturing, which decreased by 0.4% in February 2026. Pharmaceuticals fell more, at 8.6%, while motor vehicles increased by 2.0% and basic metals declined by 0.7%.

France reported the only increase at 1.1%, followed by Belgium with the smallest decline at 1.2%. The Netherlands experienced the steepest decline at 9.6%, followed by Italy (6.9%) and Germany (3.5%). Spain and Poland posted decreases of 3.3% and 2.1% respectively. These chemical production trends by country show a fragmented Europe.
