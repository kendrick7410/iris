## EU27 chemical prices decline by 2.8% in February 2026

EU27 chemical producer prices decreased by 2.8% in February 2026 compared to February 2025, reaching an index level of 117.2. In the first two months of 2026, chemical prices averaged 117.0, down by 0.7% compared to the same period in 2025.

Chemical prices declined more than total manufacturing, which edged up by 0.2% in February 2026. By contrast, basic metals prices surged by 8.8%, while pharmaceuticals increased by 1.9% and motor vehicles by 0.9%.

The Netherlands experienced the steepest price decline at 9.2%, followed by Belgium (5.5%) and France (3.0%). Germany reported a decrease of 1.7%, Spain fell by 2.8%, and Poland declined by 1.9%. Italy showed the smallest decline at 0.3%. These chemical price trends by country show a fragmented Europe.
