## EU27 chemical output remains 18% below pre-crisis levels in February 2026

EU27 chemical production stood at 79.3 in February 2026 (index 2021=100, seasonally adjusted), down by 3.9% compared to February 2025. In the first two months of 2026, EU27 chemical output averaged 79.1, down by 4.1% compared to the same period in 2025. The sector remains 18.0% below pre-crisis levels (2014–2019 average).

Chemical production underperformed total manufacturing, which declined by 0.4% in February 2026. Pharmaceuticals fell more, at 8.6%, while basic metals decreased by 0.7% and motor vehicles increased by 2.0%.

The Netherlands experienced the steepest production decline at 9.6%, followed by Italy (6.9%) and Germany (3.5%). Spain decreased by 3.3%, Poland by 2.1% and Belgium by 1.2%. France gained 1.1%. These chemical production trends by country show a fragmented Europe.
