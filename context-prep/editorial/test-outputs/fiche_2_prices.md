## EU27 chemical prices down 2.8% in February 2026

In February 2026, EU27 chemical producer prices decreased by 2.8% compared to February 2025, reaching an index level of 117.2. Chemical prices underperformed total manufacturing, which edged up by 0.2% over the same period.

The price decline was widespread across major EU27 chemical-producing countries. With 126.4, Belgium reported the highest price index level, followed by Poland (118.4) and Germany (119.0). However, Belgium also recorded the steepest decline at 5.5%, while the Netherlands experienced the sharpest drop at 9.2%. Italy showed the most resilient performance with prices down by only 0.3%.

These chemical price trends by country show a fragmented Europe.
