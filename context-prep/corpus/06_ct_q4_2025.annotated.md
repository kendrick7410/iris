# Annotation — Chemical Trends Report Q4 2025

## Phrases d'ouverture caractéristiques

1. "The EU27 chemical industry is facing a tough reality" — titre-diagnostic du summary, plus direct que Q3
2. "The competitiveness of the sector in Europe remains far below pre-crisis levels (2014-2019 average) driven by a combination of weak demand, global trade pressure and uncompetitive energy prices." — triple causalité dans une seule phrase
3. "The business trade environment in which European chemical companies are operating is exposed to high risks due to the unprecedented, global trade disruptions caused by US tariffs." — premier rapport à nommer les tarifs US comme cause directe
4. "As we approach four years since the onset of the recession (March 2026), the persistently low level of business confidence remains a core concern of the chemical sector in Europe." — ancrage temporel dramatisé ("four years")

## Commentaires de graphique

1. "This modest increase was driven primarily by a rise in current order-book levels, which supported the positive trend observed in the last two months of 2025." — causalité explicite, "modest" = calibration
2. "This highlights a clear disconnect between chemical production and overall manufacturing output." — phrase analytique de synthèse, pas dans le registre habituel (plus interprétative)
3. "Zooming into the country data analysis shows that leading EU27 countries experienced different growth rates" — "Zooming into" = transition narrative rare, presque informelle

## Tournures de comparaison temporelle

1. "in 2025 in comparison to the same period in 2024" — identique à Q3, formule figée
2. "in the first eleven months of 2025 compared to the same period in 2024" — fenêtre élargie (11 mois vs 8)
3. "down by €7.3 bn compared to 2024" — delta absolu, même structure

## Formulations de nuance / prudence

1. "rose slightly in December 2025 for the second consecutive month" — "slightly" + "second consecutive month" = amélioration qualifiée
2. "forecasts for 2026 are modest" — "modest" = prudence calibrée

## Paragraphe de conclusion-type

Même pattern que Q3 : la dernière section (trade deficit in volume tripled) se termine sur un constat chiffré brut. "Net, trade deficit for EU27 chemicals more than tripled from 2024 to 2025 (Jan-Oct)." — phrase-choc de clôture, factuelle.
