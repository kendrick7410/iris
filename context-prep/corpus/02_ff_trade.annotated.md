# Annotation — Facts & Figures 2025: Trade Development

## Phrases d'ouverture caractéristiques

1. "The global trade of chemicals benefits all partners and citizens by stimulating competition, innovation, and production efficiency." — ouverture par un principe économique, pas un chiffre
2. "The EU27 chemical industry, a key player in the global market, has historically enjoyed a significant trade surplus, averaging €43 bn in the past decade (2011-2021)."
3. "In the past years, EU27 chemicals surplus has experienced dramatic fluctuations; having plummeted from €35.5 billion in 2021 to €3.2 billion in 2022, before bouncing back to €35.1 bn in 2023, reaching almost 2021 levels."

## Commentaires de graphique

1. "2024 showed better results, and the EU27 chemicals trade surplus amounted to €47.3 bn." — verbe neutre "amounted to", chiffre précis au dixième
2. "between 2014 and 2024, the share of chemicals from China in EU27 imports has increased from about 9% to 18%." — fourchette temporelle + doublement implicite
3. "Europe's reliance on foreign suppliers has increased" — constat analytique sans jugement de valeur

## Tournures de comparaison temporelle

1. "averaging €43 bn in the past decade (2011-2021)" — moyenne décennale comme ancre
2. "having plummeted from €35.5 billion in 2021 to €3.2 billion in 2022, before bouncing back to €35.1 bn in 2023" — arc narratif en trois temps (chute-rebond-récupération)
3. "2004 vs 2024" — dans les titres H3

## Formulations de nuance / prudence

1. "reaching almost 2021 levels" — "almost" pour ne pas surestimer la reprise
2. "shows signs of serious deterioration" — "signs of" = prudence ; "serious" = qualification calibrée

## Paragraphe de conclusion-type

Pas de paragraphe de conclusion. Le dernier takeaway ("The trade position of upstream sub-sectors shows signs of serious deterioration") fonctionne comme un diagnostic de clôture — le plus grave vient en dernier.
