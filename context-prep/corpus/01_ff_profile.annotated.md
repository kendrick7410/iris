# Annotation — Facts & Figures 2025: Profile

## Phrases d'ouverture caractéristiques

1. "The European chemical industry represents around 635 billion euro in turnover and is an essential element of most everyday products."
2. "The EU27 chemical industry, encompassing base, specialty, and consumer chemicals, is vital to the European economy, supplying diverse sectors and employing 1.2 million people."
3. "The industry faces increasing challenges due to China's dominance, holding 45% of global sales and driving the majority of investments from the global chemical industry."

## Commentaires de graphique (phrase qui suit un chiffre)

1. "The specialty chemicals and petrochemicals sub-segments contribute to more than half of chemical sales." — chiffre implicite (>50%), constat structurel neutre
2. "Europe's market share on the global chemical market has declined by 8 percentage points over the past 15 years, from 21% to 13% between 2009 and 2024 in nominal sales." — double ancrage temporel + magnitude précise
3. "In contrast, China grew its global market share from 24% to 46% over the same period." — miroir comparatif immédiat

## Tournures de comparaison temporelle

1. "2004 vs 2024" — dans les titres de graphique, comparaison structurelle longue
2. "slightly decreased in 2024 vs 2023" — YoY atténué par "slightly"
3. "After one decade, foreign sales account for one third of EU27 chemical sales" — temporalité narrative, pas chiffrée

## Formulations de nuance / prudence

1. "around 635 billion euro" — "around" pour arrondir
2. "slightly decreased" — atténuation systématique quand le mouvement est faible

## Paragraphe de conclusion-type

Pas de conclusion explicite dans ce chapitre. Le dernier takeaway ("Global chemical sales 2024 are 3.6 times higher than in 2004") joue le rôle de clôture en élargissant la perspective au marché mondial — technique de zoom-out.
