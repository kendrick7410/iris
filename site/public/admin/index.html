Welcome

You're editing the Iris monthly report

Every month, the Iris data pipeline drafts a report on the European chemical industry from Eurostat and Comext sources. This is where that draft gets a human pass before publication. Your edits commit directly to the site; changes go live in about two minutes.

Economic review The Cefic Economic Analysis team confirms that the figures, framing, and causal language hold up.
Editorial review The Cefic Communications team polishes tone, clarity, and alignment with Cefic voice.

Three rules to keep in mind

  1. Do not change the numbers. Figures like "42.1%" or "€34.5 bn" are tied to the charts and to the underlying Eurostat data. If a number looks wrong, flag it to Jonathan rather than editing it here.
  2. Do not touch the chart tags or the "---" separators. They structure the dashboard views. Rephrase around them freely, but leave them intact.
  3. Tick "Reviewed" when you are done. Once ticked, the next pipeline run preserves your edits instead of overwriting them.
